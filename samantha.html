SAMANTHA RUTH PRABHU

Samantha Ruth Prabhu is an Indian actress known for her work in Telugu and Tamil cinema. Born on April 28, 1987, in Chennai, she made her acting debut with Ye Maaya Chesave (2010) and gained fame with films like Eega, Theri, Mahanati, and Family Man 2 (web series). She has won several awards, including Filmfare Awards
